INTRODUCTION TO PROJECT

In 2017, the Bath Choral Society enlisted the services of designer Stewart Henley to update their web presence, as well as create print and video materials for advertising purposes. The main objectives of this project were to showcase the diversity of the choral group and to create a cohesive and engaging brand image that would appeal to both existing and potential members, as well as audiences and sponsors. The result was a visually stunning website, promotional videos, and print materials that perfectly captured the spirit and ethos of the Bath Choral Society.

PROJECT TASKS

Stewart Henley asked me to support this project in a multimedia capacity. I worked with the photographer to create an on-location studio where he could take photos and videos. This involved setting up appropriate lighting, backdrops, and other equipment to create a professional-looking studio environment.

Once the shoot was complete, I edited the raw videos and photos to ensure that they were of the highest quality and matched the client's vision for the project. This included color correction, cropping, and other adjustments to ensure that the final product was polished and professional.

Finally, I liaised with the lead designer to ensure that all the materials were provided in the correct format for his needs. This involved creating different versions of the videos and photos, as well as providing the necessary file types for the website and print materials. The end result was a successful project that showcased the talent and diversity of the Bath Choral Society.
