INTRODUCTION TO PROJECT

This was a long-term undertaking aimed at showcasing the full range of works created by the artist Colin Crumplin. Initially, the website was designed as a simple contact landing page, but it soon evolved into a comprehensive online catalog of his entire collection, spanning over 54 years of his professional career.

Artist old Website

PROJECT TASKS

Colin Crumplin, the head of the Fine Art department at Bath Spa University, initially approached me to create a website with his contact information. He wanted a unique look, not a templated solution like many of the websites available at the time.

Over the next few years, I expanded the site to be a full library of his works, with a thumbnail gallery that accurately reflected the size and scale of the pieces. From the gallery, users could click on the images to view detailed photographs.

After learning new skills in HTML and CSS, I offered to update the website using a more modern back end. I replaced the outdated frames and tables, and implemented more contemporary methods for resizing on mobile devices. Style sheets were established for quicker updates to the site's appearance.

The updated website is not yet live as Colin is currently touring with his works and wishes to keep his current site until after the tour.
